All About Penguins

Penguins are flightless birds that live in one of the coldest parts of our world, Antartica. There are many different kinds of penguins. Here are a few listed with a fun fact or their characterizing feature:

  1. African Penguin
    • Only species native to Africa
  2. King Penguin
    • Second largest penguin species
  3. Adélie Penguin
    • Littlest penguin species in the Antarctic
  4. Fairy Penguin
    • Blue eyes and feathers
  5. Chinstrap Penguin
    • Most common penguin species
  6. Rockhopper Penguin
    • Yellow feather crests
  7. Emperor Penguin
    • Largest and tallest pengin species
  8. Gentoo Penguin
    • Bright orange bills
